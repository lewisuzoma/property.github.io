How it Works

Our Guaranteed Rent Process.

Here is how it works

Property Assessment

We start by conducting a free, no-obligation valuation of your property to determine the guaranteed rental income we can offer. Based on the assessment, we provide you with a competitive guaranteed rent offer, tailored to your property’s location and condition..

Agreement

Once you accept our offer, we sign a straightforward agreement outlining the guaranteed rent amount and the terms of our service. Our contracts are flexible, with terms ranging from 1 to 5 years, allowing you to choose what best suits your needs..

Property Management

We take care of finding and vetting tenants for your property, ensuring they meet our high standards. We handle all aspects of property management, including rent collection, maintenance, and compliance with legal requirements.

Monthly Payments

You receive your guaranteed rent payment on the same date every month, without any deductions. Even if the property is vacant or the tenant fails to pay, you still get paid.

End of Term

At the end of the contract, we return your property in its original condition (subject to fair wear and tear), or we can discuss renewing the agreement if you wish to continue.
